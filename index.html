What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency without a central bank or single administrator. It can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer Bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries.

How Bitcoin Works

Bitcoin uses blockchain technology to record transactions. A blockchain is a distributed ledger enforced by a network of computers (called nodes). When someone sends Bitcoin, the transaction is verified and recorded on the blockchain.

Advantages of Bitcoin

  • Decentralized and transparent
  • Low transaction fees
  • Global and borderless
  • Secure and immutable
